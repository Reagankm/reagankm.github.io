History

As a young man Rick Middlebrook had an idea for the perfect company name, Aces Four. If he ever founded a company he knew that’s what he would name it. In 1978 he found his calling and quit his job as an inspector with the City of Seattle to found Aces Four Construction Company with Peggy Middlebrook. Although we’ve outgrown the early days, when our one backhoe had to be driven on surface streets to reach the next job site with Peggy following behind in her Volkswagen Beetle with the four way flashers on, we’ve had the same values since day one: honesty, integrity, and courtesy.
